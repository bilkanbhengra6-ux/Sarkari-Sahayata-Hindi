YouTube Shorts Se Paisa Kamane Ka Tarika 2026

YouTube Shorts ab paise kamane ka ek bahut bada zariya ban chuka hai. Agar aap bina face dikhaye paise kamana chahte hain, toh AI tools ka use karke viral shorts bana sakte hain.

Earning Ke Tarike:

  • YouTube Ad Revenue: Jab aapke shorts viral hote hain, toh YouTube ads ka paisa deta hai.
  • Brand Collaboration: 10k followers hone par brands aapko promotion ke liye paise dete hain.
  • Affiliate Marketing: Shorts ke description mein product links de kar commission kamayein.
