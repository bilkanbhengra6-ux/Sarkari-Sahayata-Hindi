Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana: Beti Ka Bhavishya Surakshit Karein

Beti ki padhai aur shadi ke liye paison ki chinta chodiye. SSY yojana mein aapko milta hai sabse zyada byaaj (interest) aur income tax mein bhari chhoot.

Kaise khulwayein khata?

Aap apne nazdiki Post Office ya kisi bhi sarkari bank mein jakar apni beti (10 saal se kam umra) ke naam par ye khata khulwa sakte hain.
