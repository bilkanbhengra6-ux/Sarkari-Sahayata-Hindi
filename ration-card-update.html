One Nation One Ration Card: Ration Card Update 2026

Sarkar ne 'One Nation One Ration Card' yojana ke tehat ab pure desh mein kahin se bhi ration lena aasaan kar diya hai. Agar aapne apna card update nahi kiya hai, toh aapka ration ruk sakta hai.

Kaise karein update?

  • NFSA ki official website par jayein.
  • Apna Ration Card number aur Aadhaar link karein.
  • E-KYC poora karein taaki har mahine milne wala anaaj milta rahe.
