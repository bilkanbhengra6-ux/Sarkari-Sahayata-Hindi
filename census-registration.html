Digital Census 2026: Ghar Baithe Janganana Me Apni Jankari Kaise Bharein?

Bharat Sarkar ne ab Digital Census (Janganana) ki shuruat kar di hai. Is baar aapko lambe intezar ki zaroorat nahi hai, aap khud "Self-Enumeration" portal ke zariye apni aur apne parivaar ki jankari online darj kar sakte hain.

Slogan: "India is getting counted and so are you - Are you prepared?"

Census Online Registration Ke Fayde

  • Aapko kisi sarkari daftar jane ki zaroorat nahi hai.
  • Jankari puri tarah se gupt (secure) rakhi jati hai.
  • Mobile ya Laptop se 5 minute me process pura kar sakte hain.

Apply Kaise Karein? (Step-by-Step)

  1. Sabse pehle niche diye gaye Official Portal link par click karein.
  2. Apna Mobile Number daalkar OTP verify karein.
  3. Apne parivaar ke sadasyon ki sankhya aur basic details bharein.
  4. Submit karne ke baad aapko ek Reference Number milega, ise sambhal kar rakhein.
